About Us

Based in Santa Inez, Goa, Central Perk is a family owned Mediterranean café, blending traditional recipes with a modern flair.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Mexican, and American culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The café boasts a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular spot for meals throughout the day.

Central Perk is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to India to fulfill their shared dream of running a café.

To craft the menu, Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy.

Adrian does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the mediterranean region.
